About Me

I am Technologist, Mentor and Evangelist. I provide direction to Organizations on the technology and leadership side of things. I am in the business of creating more leaders, giving tech direction to the Organizations since last 12+ years. Currently I am playing the role of Director of Engineering at Synerzip. I bring 3 things to the table and these are -
 

Tech Leadership

Technologies come and go. My role is to evaluate them, predict what opportunities lies in future with these technologies. I advice companies on what to bank on and how to prepare for them. Doing External facing Webinars and Internal Tech Summits is part of my role.

Evangelism

Technology, Culture and Fun spread based on how you Evangelize. I play a role of an Evangelist not only for Technology but the kind of culture a hyperactive team needs to deliver the best product and customer delight. And having fun while doing that is my prerequiste.

Leadership

A Leaders role is to create more Leaders. Identifying People's ambitions, their strengths and mentoring them to reach their full potential is what I have been doing for past decade. I come in, setup the team, seed leaders and when things are ripe, I step out.
